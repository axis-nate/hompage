AXIS is not a general practice that happens to handle digital assets. It is a specialty practice built entirely around digital assets, AI, Web3, and cross-border structuring — by an attorney who has personally executed every phase of a digital asset company's lifecycle, from formation through wind-down.

Combined with Korean and U.S. dual-jurisdiction legal expertise, direct policy access through KWBA, and a global alliance network spanning Hong Kong, Europe, and beyond — AXIS is a single point of entry for clients navigating the digital asset landscape.
